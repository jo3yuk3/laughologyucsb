Laughology is UCSB's groundbreaking standup comedy club.

Every Saturday Laughology features hilarious comedians who can be seen on networks such as HBO, Comedy Central, NBC, and Netflix. All performers are regulars at the top clubs such as The Laugh Factory and the Hollywood Improv.

Always Free, Always 8pm, Always Embarcadero Hall.


In addition to shows, we also help aspiring student comedians learn how to do standup comedy and eventually have members perorm as opening acts in our weekly shows. We host regular open mic events around Isla Vista for students to get their feet wet.

If you're interested in joining check us out on Facebook or Instagram.
